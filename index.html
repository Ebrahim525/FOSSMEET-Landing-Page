About FOSSMEET

FOSSMeet is an annual event on Free and Open Source Software at National Institute of Technology Calicut. It is a gathering of open-source enthusiasts, developers, and learners who believe in the power of collaborative technology. Explore ideas, participate in exciting events, and connect with the community.
